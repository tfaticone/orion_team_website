  • Multi-Layer Chess (With some rule changes listed below):
    • You get The full number of Pieces for each board.
    • You may arrange the pieces anyway you wish in your area. However, each level must contain a King. There are no restrictions other then that.
    • You only get a single move each turn, not a single turn on each bored.
    • When you capture an enemy's piece, with the exception of the Enemy's King, you get a copy of that piece in your "unit reserves".
    • Instead of moving a piece during your turn, you may instead place a piece from your "Unit Reserves" onto any board within your "zone".
    • When an enemy's King is captured, that board becomes inactive along with all pieces on it, and you gain a Point.
    • The winner is the person who has earned the most points by the time all of the boards become inactive.
    • For clarification, inactive pieces are that, inactive. An inactive peice can not be used again during the game.
